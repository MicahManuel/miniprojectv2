Places to Visit

GATEWAY

Visit Gateway Square (Quezon City) for a comprehensive lifestyle destination offering extensive shopping and dining, entertainment,games/arcades at Gateway Cineplex 18, cultural experiences at Gateway Gallery, and connectivity to Araneta City and the Smart Araneta Coliseum. The complex provides diverse culinary options and features amenities such as open-air gardens and direct links to public transportation.

ART IN ISLAND

Visit Art in Island in Quezon City to experience unique,entertaining, interactive 3D art and immersive media displays that are perfect for photos and creating lasting memories. The museum offers a creative playground with optical illusions, light shows, and animated projections, providing a fun and engaging experience for families and friends. Visitors are encouraged to photograph themselves to become "part" of the compositions.

UP - DILIMAN

Visit UP Diliman in Quezon City to explore its numerous cultural sites, museums, and performances on its expansive campus, enjoy green spaces like the UP Lagoon and Sunken Garden, or experience dining and shopping at the adjacent UP Town Center. The university is also a center for groundbreaking research and a symbol of academic excellence in the Philippines, making it a worthwhile destination for cultural and intellectual enrichment.

MEMORIAL CIRCLE

Visit Memorial Circle in Quezon City to honor President Quezon at his shrine and museum, find historical and cultural sites like the Quezon Heritage House, enjoy the park's green spaces, gardens, and recreational facilities such as a playground and bike rentals, and experience the local atmosphere with food and shops. The park also features the World Peace Bell and serves as a vibrant public space for relaxation, exercise, and community activities.
